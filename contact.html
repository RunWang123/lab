Prospective Students

  • Prospective PhD students: We are looking for motivated PhD students, especially those from underrepresented groups, to join our lab. Please apply to Clemson CS PhD program and indicate your interest in working with Dr. Siyu Huang. Feel free to email Dr. Siyu Huang (siyuh@clemson.edu) your CV with "PhD application - Your name" as the subject.
  • Current Clemson students, especially those from underrepresented groups, are encouraged to reach out to our lab members for discussions on collaborations. We are open to collaborations both inside and outside the School of Computing at Clemson University.
  • Visiting students, research interns: We are looking for visiting students and research interns! Feel free to email Dr. Siyu Huang (siyuh@clemson.edu) your CV with "Intern application - Your name" as the subject.
